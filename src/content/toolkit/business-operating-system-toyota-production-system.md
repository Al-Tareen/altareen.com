## When to use
Management philosophy developed by Toyota that focuses on eliminating waste and improving efficiency in manufacturing. It is based on two main principles: Just-in-Time production, which means producing only what is needed when it is needed, and Jidoka, which emphasizes automation with a human touch to ensure quality.
