## When to use
When experimenting or shipping AI and you need to prevent harm, trust loss, or cost blowups.

## Inputs required
Failure modes; harm model; cost/latency constraints; escalation policy.

## Output artifact
Guardrails + thresholds + alerts + escalation triggers.

## Common mistakes
No thresholds; guardrails not monitored; alert noise; guardrails ignored in decisions.
