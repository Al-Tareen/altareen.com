## When to use
When you’re trying to decide “which customer segment do we serve first?” (and you can’t justify it).

## Inputs required
Market map; user/customer data; WTP/budget signals; constraints (integration/regulatory); channel access.

## Output artifact
Segment definitions; ICP selection rationale; “why now” wedge segment; implications for product/GTM.

## Common mistakes
Segments by demographics only; too many segments; choosing “everyone”; no WTP validation.
