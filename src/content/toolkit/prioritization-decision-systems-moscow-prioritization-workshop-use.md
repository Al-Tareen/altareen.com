## When to use
When stakeholders can’t agree on scope and you need to force “must vs nice-to-have” decisions.

## Inputs required
Candidate scope list; constraints (time/people); dependency map; stakeholder attendance.

## Output artifact
Must/Should/Could/Won’t list; release scope agreement.

## Common mistakes
Everything becomes “Must”; no constraint enforcement; no decision owner.
