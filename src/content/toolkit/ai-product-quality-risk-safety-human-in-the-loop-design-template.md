## When to use
When the model is not reliable enough to fully automate and a human must approve or correct outputs.

## Inputs required
Decision points; reviewer roles; SLA expectations; UI workflow; audit needs.

## Output artifact
HITL workflow spec; responsibility boundaries; QA gates; audit trail plan.

## Common mistakes
Human as rubber stamp; unclear accountability; bottlenecks; no audit trail.
