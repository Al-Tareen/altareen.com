## When to use
When an AI feature touches sensitive data, high-stakes decisions, or could be misused.

## Inputs required
Use case description; data types; user harm analysis; threat scenarios; compliance requirements.

## Output artifact
Risk register; mitigations; risk owners; sign-off criteria.

## Common mistakes
Checkbox theater; ignores misuse; no owner per risk; no thresholds.
