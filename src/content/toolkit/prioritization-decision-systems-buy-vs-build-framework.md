## When to use
When deciding whether to integrate a vendor/model or build your own capability.

## Inputs required
Requirements; security/compliance; TCO; integration effort; lock-in risk; timeline.

## Output artifact
Recommendation; TCO; integration plan; risks + mitigations; exit plan.
