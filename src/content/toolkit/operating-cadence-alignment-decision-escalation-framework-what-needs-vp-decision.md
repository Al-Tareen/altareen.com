## When to use
When a decision is blocked and you need a clear path to get it decided at the right level.

## Inputs required
Decision type; owner; impact; risk; deadline; options + recommendation.

## Output artifact
Escalation path + SLA + decision package format.

## Common mistakes
Escalating too early/late; unclear owner; vague framing; no recommendation.
